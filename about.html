about

Launched by Jacob Borg, ecoBorg is a  Christian, conservationist design studio that prioritizes  quality, affordability, and environmental impact.

Origins

Jake was originally hesitant to create a studio, but his friends insisted. So he gave into their demands, and launched a Christian, eco-friendly design studio.

values

Created with a purpose, for a purpose.

stewardship

As dictated in Genesis 1:26, we have a duty to tend to His Creation. For us, that looks like reducing our waste, using renewable energy, and leaving everywhere we go cleaner than how we found it.

faith

Our Christian faith is the foundation of our entire business. We strive to make the world a better place by serving Him, sharing the Gospel, and tending to the Earth.

innovation

We strive to innovate and create in everything we do. Whether it is a new website, print ad, or even new technology, we are always looking to innovate and create.
