Conservation

Per our faith, we believe that we as Christians have a duty to tend to God's creation and not abuse it. With this belief, we strive to have a positive impact on the environment.

reducing waste

The design process, from ideation to production, has historically involved a lot of paper.  We are cutting out paper from our design  ideation process by utilizing reusable products such as Rocket Books, white boards,  and digital tools.

eco-hosting

All hosting, either from us or  our third-party services, is eco-friendly. This is done via renewable-powered servers,  off-setting, and reducing energy consumption.
